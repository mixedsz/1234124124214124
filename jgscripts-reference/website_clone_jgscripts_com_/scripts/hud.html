🎨 Ultra-Customisable

And we mean ultra. Choose from 6 unique speedometer designs (including EV-specific layouts), 3 bicycle styles, 2 for boats, 2 for aircraft, 3 compass types, 4 health display styles, 4 player info panels - even a dedicated train HUD! Every component can be individually moved, resized, re-coloured and styled. Many designs are split into parts for finer control. We’re adding more styles regularly, based on community feedback.

🗺️ Minimap, Reimagined

Your minimap now has the same flexibility as the rest of your HUD. Drag it anywhere on screen, click and drag to resize it from any corner, and even shift it outside the safezone. On ultrawide displays, you can drag it from edge to edge. You can even transform it from the classic rectangle to a circular GTA IV-style map. You have to experience it to believe it.

✅ Standalone or Framework-Ready

Run JG HUD standalone out of the box for custom servers, race servers, or vMenu setups. Or integrate with QBCore, Qbox, or ESX to show job, gang, money, hunger and thirst. It also supports voice and radio display via pma-voice. Every integration is open and unobfuscated for full control.

🤩 Use your Custom Configuration Server-Wide

Craft the perfect HUD setup - layout, styles, colours, minimap - and export to JSON in a click. You can use the JSON to set it as a server-wide configuration and lock further editing to maintain your vision. Thanks to JG HUD’s AutoScale tech, your layout adapts perfectly to any resolution or aspect ratio, from standard widescreen to 21:9 and 32:9 ultrawides.

↔️ Import & Export Configurations

Build a layout you love? Bring it with you. Export your setup to JSON and import it on other servers using JG HUD, or share it with friends. AutoScale ensures layouts look exactly as the creator intended, regardless of resolution, and even on ultrawides.

🖥️ Built for Ultrawide

At last, a HUD that works flawlessly on every aspect ratio. With an increasing number of people playing on 21:9 and 32:9 ultrawide displays, you can ensure your server stands out. Whether players want to keep elements tight in their field of view or expand their safezone across their ultrawide display, JG HUD handles both seamlessly. We tested everything - including minimap movement in and out of the safezone. We even bought an ultrawide monitor just to get it right.

🎛️ Integrated Vehicle Controls

A full vehicle control panel comes built in, by default via F6. Toggle the engine, doors, windows, seats, and advanced options like indicators, hazards, boat anchor, landing gear, cruise control, seatbelt or convertible roof. It’s synced too - if a passenger opens a door, your panel reflects it in real time. As always, it’s fully moveable, resizable and can be re-coloured to match your server’s theme.

🔫 Weapon HUD

See your weapon’s name with a sleek icon, plus current clip and total ammo. You can hide it or restyle it with your own colour, shadow, size and position on the screen.

✉️ Nearest Postals

We’ve integrated DevBlocky’s Nearest Postals directly into the HUD, showing the nearest postal and distance from within the compass itself. It supports popular data sets like OCRP’s postals right out of the box. Use it with or without the original resource running - your choice.

🛑 Smart Speed Limits

Display speed limits while driving - US or UK style. By default, the display subtly flashes if the player exceeds the limit. Most roads in Los Santos are already configured with their speed limits, and you can adjust them easily for your own requirements.

🚗 QoL Vehicle Features

We’ve packed in quality of life tools: an ultra lightweight, server-synced indicator system, seatbelts, cruise control and boat anchor. All are usable via keybinds or the vehicle control panel - whichever you prefer.

📈 Blazing Fast Performance

JG HUD is extremely lightweight, measuring 0.01ms idle and 0.02–0.03ms while driving in resmon. Some lower-end CPUs typically report 0.02 idle and 0.04–0.06ms in-vehicle. We’re constantly refining performance, using the latest natives and best practices to reduce the impact of the client threads.

🧠 Optional Addon: Stress System

If you’re migrating from a HUD with stress functionality, we’ve got you covered. Our open-source addon isolates the awesome qbx_hud stress system as a standalone module. No setup needed - just drag and drop. Click here to download from GitHub.
