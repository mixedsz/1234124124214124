About

Our Story

JG Scripts builds premium vehicle gameplay systems and server tools for FiveM roleplay servers. The company was founded by software developer James in 2022 and focuses on highly scalable vehicle systems such as JG Mechanic, JG Dealerships, JG Advanced Garages and JG HUD.

JG Scripts products are used by over 8,000 FiveM servers worldwide and have achieved more than 40,000 sales. Scripts are compatible with the major FiveM frameworks: QBCore, Qbox & ESX.

We focus on performance, security and usability. Our systems are designed for large server populations, use secure server events, maintain low resmon usage, and feature modern intuitive user interfaces.
